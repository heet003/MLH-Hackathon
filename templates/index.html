About FIR

FIRST INFORMATION REPORT, commonly known as FIR, is a legal document that is filed with the police in India when a cognizable offence is committed. It is the first document that is registered by the police and sets the process of criminal justice in motion. An FIR provides the details of the offence, the identity of the accused and the complainant, and any witnesses or evidence.
